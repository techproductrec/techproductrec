Acer Hit Series Smart Android TV Review

Introduction:

Acer has recently launched their Hit Series Smart Android TVs, which are positioned as a slightly premium category offering from the brand. These TVs promise a range of impressive features at competitive price points.

Features:

  • Available in three sizes: 43-inch, 50-inch, and 55-inch.
  • Supports Dolby Vision, Dolby Atmos, and HDR 10, with MEMC support.
  • Powered by Android 11 operating system with OK Google integration.
  • Competitive pricing, with the 55-inch variant priced at only 33,999 INR.

Unboxing and Accessories:

The TV comes in a simple yet sturdy box packaging. Inside, you'll find:

  • TV remote with compact and stylish design.
  • Two AAA batteries for the remote.
  • Main power cord.
  • 3.5mm to RCA cable.
  • Installation screws.
  • Stylish and compact metallic TV stands.

Design and Build Quality:

The Acer Hit Series TV features a sleek design with three-sided narrow bezels, giving it a modern look. The bezels are thin, except for the bottom chin, which has a metallic finish. The stands are stylish, made entirely of metal, adding to the overall premium feel of the TV.

Connectivity:

The back of the TV has a minimalistic design with good build quality. It offers a variety of connectivity options, including:

  • Two USB ports (USB 2.0 and USB 3.0).
  • Ethernet LAN port.
  • RF cable in.
  • Optical out.
  • Three HDMI ports (one with eARC support).
  • Headphone jack and mini AVN.

Software and User Interface:

The TV runs on the latest Android 11 operating system, providing access to a wide range of apps and games via the Google Play Store. The home screen is user-friendly, with easy access to favorite apps like Netflix, YouTube, Prime Video, and more.

Picture and Sound Quality:

The Acer Hit Series TV offers impressive picture quality with vibrant colors, wide color gamut support, and good contrast levels. It supports HDR 10+ and Dolby Vision, enhancing the viewing experience for compatible content. The sound quality is exceptional, thanks to the 60-watt Hi-Fi stereo speakers, providing clear and detailed audio with decent bass.

Performance:

The TV delivers smooth performance, with no lags or stutters during navigation and app usage. It has sufficient RAM and storage for multitasking and app installations. The motion compensation feature ensures smooth playback of content, especially for fast-paced action scenes.

Remote and Additional Features:

The TV remote is compact and ergonomic, with dedicated buttons for popular OTT platforms and built-in Google Assistant support. While the TV lacks specific gaming-related features, it offers an overall excellent value proposition for its price range.

Conclusion:

The Acer Hit Series Smart Android TV impresses with its sleek design, robust build quality, and a wide range of features, including Dolby Vision, Dolby Atmos, and HDR 10 support. With competitive pricing starting at 25,999 INR, it offers excellent value for money and is recommended for those seeking a premium TV experience without breaking the bank.
