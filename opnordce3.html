OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite 5G


In a market filled with competitive offerings, OnePlus has introduced the Nord CE 3 Lite aimed at budget-conscious consumers. Priced at Rs 19,999, this device aims to stand out in a crowded segment. Let's explore its features and performance.

Design and Build Quality: The OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite sports a design that's somewhat familiar yet distinctive with its two large camera rings on the back. The glossy plastic back panel, while visually appealing, is prone to scratches. The inclusion of a 3.5mm headphone jack is a notable feature, but the absence of the alert slider might disappoint some users.

Display: With a 6.7-inch LCD panel offering a 120Hz refresh rate and FHD+ resolution, the display provides decent viewing angles. However, its peak brightness of 680 nits falls short for outdoor usage. Additionally, slight backlight bleeding around the punch-hole camera affects display quality.

Camera Performance: Featuring an upgraded 108MP primary camera along with 2MP macro and depth sensors, the Nord CE 3 Lite delivers satisfactory results in daylight photography. However, low-light performance and focus accuracy are areas that could see improvement. The absence of an ultra-wide sensor is notable.

Performance and Software: Powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 695G chipset, the Nord CE 3 Lite offers decent performance for everyday tasks. OxygenOS 13.1, based on Android 13, provides a clean user interface with minimal bloatware.

Battery and Charging: Equipped with a 5,000mAh battery and 67W SuperVOOC charging, the device offers respectable battery life and fast charging speeds.

Final Verdict: The OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite is a modest upgrade over its predecessor, focusing on design aesthetics and multimedia experience. While it excels in certain areas like design and battery life, its camera performance and display quality may leave some users wanting more.

Pros: Attractive design Impressive stereo speakers Clean user interface Respectable battery life

Cons: Subpar camera performance LCD display falls short in outdoor visibility

In conclusion, the OnePlus Nord CE 3 Lite offers a compelling package for budget-conscious consumers but may not satisfy those seeking superior camera capabilities and display quality.
